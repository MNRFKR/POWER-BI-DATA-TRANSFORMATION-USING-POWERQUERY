Merge vs Append in Power BI?
- Merge combines columns from two tables based on a key (like joining FirstName from one table with Department from another).
- Append stacks rows from two tables with the same structure (like adding more employee records to the same dataset).

Why does datatype matter in BI?
Correct datatypes (e.g., Date, Text, Number) ensure visuals, filters, and calculations behave as expected. For example, converting DOB to Date enabled age calculations, and fixing PayZone as Text allowed proper grouping in the pie chart.

Measures vs Columns?
- Columns are stored in the data model and calculated row-by-row (e.g., AgeGroup, SalaryBand).
- Measures are dynamic calculations based on filters and visuals (e.g., Average Satisfaction Score in the bar chart).
